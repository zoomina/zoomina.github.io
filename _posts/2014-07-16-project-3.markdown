    ### Shownote Brand Analysis using Twitter + KOPIS Ticketing Data
    #Python #NLP #WebCrawling #KOPISOpenAPI #DataPreprocessing #Tableau #DataVisualization
    <br><br>
    <strong>Problem</strong>
    <ul style="list-style:none; padding-left:0;">
      <li>This project examined Shownote’s brand positioning and audience demand using both ticketing data and social media reactions.</li>
      <li>The goal was to identify what drives booking performance and what audiences expect from the brand.</li>
    </ul>
    <br>
    <strong>Data</strong>
    <ul>
      <li>KOPIS open API ticketing and occupancy data</li>
      <li>Twitter crawling data related to musicals and audience reactions</li>
    </ul>
    <br>
    <strong>Method</strong>
    <ul>
      <li>Time-series analysis of booking and occupancy trends by show/date</li>
      <li>Korean NLP-based keyword extraction and word cloud analysis</li>
      <li>Tableau dashboard for combined exploration of sales and audience demand</li>
    </ul>
    <br>
    <strong>Insights</strong>
    <ul>
      <li>Booking trends were affected by cast exposure, media appearances, and seasonal events</li>
      <li>Audience demand signals such as video and soundtrack-related needs appeared clearly in Twitter data</li>
      <li>Quantitative and qualitative signals together provided a stronger basis for marketing direction</li>
    </ul>
