    ### Shownote Brand Analysis using Twitter + KOPIS Ticketing Data
    #Python #NLP #WebCrawling #KOPISOpenAPI #DataPreprocessing #Tableau #DataVisualization
    <br>

    **Problem**

    This project examined Shownote’s brand positioning and audience demand using both ticketing data and social media reactions.  
    The goal was to identify what drives booking performance and what audiences expect from the brand.

    <br>

    **Data**

    - KOPIS open API ticketing and occupancy data  
    - Twitter crawling data related to musicals and audience reactions

    <br>

    **Method**

    - Time-series analysis of booking and occupancy trends by show/date  
    - Korean NLP-based keyword extraction and word cloud analysis  
    - Tableau dashboard for combined exploration of sales and audience demand

    <br>

    **Insights**

    - Booking trends were affected by cast exposure, media appearances, and seasonal events  
    - Audience demand signals such as video and soundtrack-related needs appeared clearly in Twitter data  
    - Quantitative and qualitative signals together provided a stronger basis for marketing direction

    <br>

    The full report covers detailed ticketing patterns and demand keywords by content.
