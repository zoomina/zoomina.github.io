    ### Validating Market Growth and Campaign Timing through Google Trends–Based Demand Analysis

    #Python #Time Series Analysis #Trend Extraction #Seasonality Decomposition #Search Demand Analysis #EDA

    <br>

    **Problem**

    This project tested whether the wearable healthcare device market showed real demand growth and when campaigns should be launched.  
    Rather than relying only on qualitative reports, it focused on validating timing with behavioral search data.

    <br>

    **Data**

    - Five years of Google Trends search volume data  
    - Search demand related to wearable healthcare devices and the Feels product category

    <br>

    **Method**

    - Time-series trend analysis  
    - Seasonal decomposition to identify recurring demand peaks  
    - Strategic interpretation of search demand for B2B and B2C timing

    <br>

    **Insights**

    - The category showed sustained growth rather than short-term hype  
    - Demand peaks appeared repeatedly in January and August  
    - Based on seasonality, the project proposed a B2B-first and B2C-expansion strategy

    <br>

    The full report explains how search behavior was translated into campaign timing decisions.

    정성적 시장 전망에만 의존하지 않고 검색 행동 데이터로 타이밍을 판단하는 데 초점을 맞췄습니다.

    - Google Trends 5년치 검색량 데이터
