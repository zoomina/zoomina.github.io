    ### Validating Market Growth and Campaign Timing through Google Trends–Based Demand Analysis
    #Python #Time Series Analysis #Trend Extraction #Seasonality Decomposition #Search Demand Analysis #EDA
    <br><br>
    <strong>Problem</strong>
    <ul style="list-style:none; padding-left:0;">
      <li>This project tested whether the wearable healthcare device market showed real demand growth and when campaigns should be launched.</li>
      <li>Rather than relying only on qualitative reports, it focused on validating timing with behavioral search data.</li>
    </ul>
    <br>
    <strong>Data</strong>
    <ul>
      <li>Five years of Google Trends search volume data</li>
      <li>Search demand related to wearable healthcare devices and the Feels product category</li>
    </ul>
    <br>
    <strong>Method</strong>
    <ul>
      <li>Time-series trend analysis</li>
      <li>Seasonal decomposition to identify recurring demand peaks</li>
      <li>Strategic interpretation of search demand for B2B and B2C timing</li>
    </ul>
    <br>
    <strong>Insights</strong>
    <ul>
      <li>The category showed sustained growth rather than short-term hype</li>
      <li>Demand peaks appeared repeatedly in January and August</li>
      <li>Based on seasonality, the project proposed a B2B-first and B2C-expansion strategy</li>
    </ul>
